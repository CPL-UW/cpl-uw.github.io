about our work

understanding how players learn with creatively play is hard because play, games, creativity, construction, and learning are all non-linear and intertwined

the complex play lab focuses on understanding and supporting creative and constructive complex play. we help kids learn by building, learn by playing, and we use innovative research tools and innovative game design to do it.
